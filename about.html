About Fox’s

Fox’s Deli & Bakery,
originally Fox’s Delicatessen, was founded in 1924 by Howard Fox.


Since then, we’ve been a proud staple of the southern New York and northern New Jersey area.


Over 100 years later, Howard’s granddaughter Wendy has taken up the helm, but Fox’s dedication to quality remains the same as ever.
